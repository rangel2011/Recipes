About Us

We both love to eat

While also spening time in the kitchen trying new things

We met each other in January of 2019. From the very first date at Salted Pig, we both knew that we were "the ones" for each other. I quickly moved into his one bedroom apartment in downtown Riverside and from there, we started to spend more time making each other food. We wanted to create a central location to find it easy to look up the recipes that we have enjoyed and also have meaning to us. So, welcome to our recipe page and you can learn more about us through food.
